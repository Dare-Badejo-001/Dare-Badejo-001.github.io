# About Dare Badejo

![Banner image for the About page showing a professional workspace](assets/images/about-banner.png){ .page-banner }

## Applied Scientist specializing in AI-driven decision systems and sustainability optimization at Dow.

I am an applied scientist working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, optimization, and sustainability. My career is defined by a single mission: bridging rigorous research, industry practice, and data-driven decision-making to solve problems that matter.

---

## Background

I am a **Scientist at Dow**, where I **develop sustainability tools and frameworks**, including **life cycle assessment (LCA), techno-economic analysis (TEA), and data-driven decision models**, helping industry leaders drive measurable environmental and operational impact.

Prior to Dow, I completed my **PhD in Chemical Engineering at the University of Delaware**, focusing on **enterprise-wide optimization under uncertainty, integrated systems modeling, agent-based frameworks, interpretable machine learning, and sustainability-driven decision systems**. My research bridges advanced analytical methods with practical applications, turning complex models into actionable insights for industrial and supply chain systems.

Earlier in my career, I gained hands-on experience in both **academia and industry**,  at the University of Lagos and Eunisell Nigeria Limited,  applying quantitative and data-driven methods to real-world process and operational challenges. These experiences cemented my ability to **translate research into impactful, real-world solutions.**
